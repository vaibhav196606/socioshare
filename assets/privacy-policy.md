# Privacy Policy for SocioShare

**Last Updated: December 10, 2025**

## Introduction

SocioShare ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our Shopify application.

## Information We Collect

### Store Information
When you install SocioShare, we access the following information from your Shopify store:
- **Store name and URL** - To configure the app for your store
- **Product information** - To enable sharing functionality (product titles, URLs, and images)

### What We DO NOT Collect
- We do not collect personal customer data
- We do not collect payment or financial information
- We do not track individual customer behavior
- We do not store customer email addresses or contact information

## How We Use Information

The information we access is used solely to:
- Provide the social sharing functionality on your product pages
- Generate correct sharing links for social media platforms
- Display product information in social media share previews

## Data Storage

- **App settings** are stored locally in your Shopify store
- **No customer data** is transmitted to or stored on our servers
- **Sharing actions** occur directly between your customers and social media platforms

## Third-Party Services

When customers click share buttons, they interact directly with third-party social media platforms:
- WhatsApp (Meta)
- Facebook (Meta)
- Twitter/X
- Pinterest
- LinkedIn

Each platform has its own privacy policy governing how they handle shared content.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your store information.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Uninstall** the app at any time, which removes all app data
- **Request information** about what data we access
- **Contact us** with any privacy concerns

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

This app complies with:
- Shopify's API Terms of Service
- GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
- CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
